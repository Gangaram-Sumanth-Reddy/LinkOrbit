Privacy Policy

Introduction Information We Collect How We Use Information Sharing Information Data Security User Rights Third Party Services Contact Information

Introduction

Welcome to Linkorbit. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our link-in-bio platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

This website serves as a personal link aggregation platform that redirects users to external services and platforms. We do not collect or store personal user data beyond basic analytics for improving user experience.

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Information We Collect

Our platform collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Analytics Data: We may collect anonymous usage data such as page views, click events, and device information to understand how users interact with the platform.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address (anonymized), and referral sources may be logged for security and performance optimization.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it through contact forms or booking services.

How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: Understanding user behavior helps us optimize the platform's design and functionality.
  • Performance Monitoring: Technical data helps us identify and fix bugs, improve loading times, and ensure platform stability.
  • Security: Monitoring traffic patterns helps us detect and prevent malicious activity or abuse.
  • Communication: If you contact us directly, we use your information solely to respond to your inquiry.

Sharing Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. Information may only be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the limited data we collect:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication for administrative functions
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have collected about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of any data we have collected (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics tracking by using browser privacy settings or ad blockers.
  • Correction: You can request correction of any inaccurate information we may have.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@codegai.com.

Third Party Services

This platform contains links to external services and platforms. When you click these links, you are redirected to third-party websites that have their own privacy policies:

  • Social Media: Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, GitHub, Discord
  • Booking Services: Cal.com for scheduling appointments
  • Email Services: Your default email client for contact
  • Analytics: Google Analytics or similar services for usage tracking

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@codegai.com

Platform: Linkorbit by Codegai

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
